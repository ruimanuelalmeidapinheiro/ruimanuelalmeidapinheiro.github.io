📊 IQ Test (General Intelligence Quotient)

The IQ Test measures multiple cognitive dimensions that contribute to overall intelligence:

  • Pattern Recognition — Identifying and categorizing sensory arrangements into organized patterns
  • Spatial Orientation — Understanding spatial relationships and object positions in space
  • Visual Perception — Organizing, processing, and interpreting visual information
  • Analytical Thinking — Dissecting information into components and developing logical solutions
  • Abstract Reasoning — Handling abstract concepts and grasping new ideas independently
