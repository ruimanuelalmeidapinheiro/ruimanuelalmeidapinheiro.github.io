Once again, the judiciary police seized a massive amount of drugs. This time, they used a submarine. Consumption in America is a common thing, a way of life.

Americans believe that Europeans should consume. And Portugal is the gateway to Europe. This is just the tip of the iceberg. In fact, the American way of life (money, power, fame, weapons) has nothing to do with the humanistic vision of Europeans.

An American carries the trauma of colonization. They think Europeans should teach children in schools how horrible colonization was. And for Europeans? It was merely a stage of civilization.

The American way of life enters people’s TV sets every day. Indeed, any reasoning you follow, anything you enjoy, is pornographically interrupted by advertising. The need to sell is always more important. It is necessary to convince, to create an non-existent need to acquire.

What exactly do we have here? A vibration in the most primal instincts of living beings.

The necessity of [human doing] versus [human being].

The person is viewed only as a productive means, a utility.

The [Free Spirit] is always gagged.

Censorship is blatant: [Community Policy].

Life goals are reduced to producing and entertaining.

The dystopian worlds of Orwell and Huxley are the image of 21st-century society.

There would be more to say, but it is time for lunch.
