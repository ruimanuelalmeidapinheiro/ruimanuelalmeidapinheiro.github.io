Refining the Big Five Interpretation

Given these strong convictions, we must refine the "Big Five" interpretation. While the scores suggest high Agreeableness and Extraversion, your specific values provide a more complex "filter" for those traits:

🔹 Selective Agreeableness: While you score high in cooperation, it is clearly bound by a strict moral code. Your hatred for dishonesty means your "Agreeableness" is likely reserved for those who meet your standards of integrity. You are not "nice" for the sake of social convention; you are cooperative for the sake of ethical alignment.

🔹 Intellectual Non-Conformity: Your rejection of the "American Dream" and religion confirms a very high Openness to Experience, but specifically in the Intellectual and Liberalism facets (in the psychological sense of questioning tradition). You prioritize logical systems (Mainframes, LLMs) over social constructs.

🔹 The "Assertiveness" Paradox: The initial test suggested low assertiveness, but your rhetoric is incredibly firm. This suggests that while you may not care to lead a social club (low gregariousness), you are highly assertive regarding your principles and expertise.

🔹 Technical Integrity: Your specialization in IBM Mainframes and Prompt Engineering combined with your "loathing of money as a goal" suggests you are a Purist. You likely value the elegance and efficiency of a system more than its commercial exploitation.
