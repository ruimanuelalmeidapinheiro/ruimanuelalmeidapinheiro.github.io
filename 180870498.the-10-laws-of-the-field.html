The 10 Laws of the Field

2025-12-06 10:29 • 1.495 words • Reading time 00:07

This post come from the Work of Helder Teixeira

Helder Teixeira on Facebook

1. LAW OF THE AXIS — The Architecture of Self

This isn’t about “being centered” as a nice idea. It’s structural mechanics. Imagine your being as a spinning gyroscope. The Axis is the central spindle. If it’s true, the gyroscope can withstand any external force, moving with purpose and stability. If it’s warped or off-center, the slightest touch sends it into a chaotic, self-destructive wobble.

  • “Everything not aligned, breaks inside.” This is cause and effect, not punishment. A thought against your truth, an action against your integrity—these are micro-fractures. They don’t just hurt; they weaken the structure. Chronic misalignment is how people shatter under pressure.

  • “The Field… reveals the path when the axle becomes stable.” The path isn’t plotted. It’s exposed, like a landmass revealed by retreating fog. Your stable axis becomes the sextant by which the Field’s topography becomes navigable. Clarity is a function of inner stability.

Deep Implication: Alignment isn’t a feeling. It’s a state of structural integrity. You don’t think your way into it. You build it through choices that honor your deepest truth, until it becomes your default posture.

2. LAW OF RESONANCE — The Physics of Attraction

Forget “positive vibes.” This is ontological. You are a broadcasting tower emitting a unique frequency signature, composed of your dominant emotional state, core beliefs, and unconscious shadow.

  • “You get what you vibe, not what you ask for.” Your sincere prayer for abundance, emitted from a frequency of scarcity, only amplifies the scarcity signature. The Field is literal. It matches output, not request.

  • “What you hide, you also attract.” This is the brutal clause. The repressed rage, the unacknowledged shame—they broadcast on a sub-carrier wave. You then attract situations and people that resonate with that hidden frequency, forcing you to confront what you buried. Your life becomes a mirror of your complete signal, seen and unseen.

Deep Implication: Personal alchemy is the work of consciously shifting your core frequency, not just changing your words. This requires transforming the hidden broadcasts (Shadow Work - Law 6).

3. LAW OF PRESENCE — The Currency of Creation

Presence is the required energy for transaction within the Field. A “half-wish” is like offering counterfeit currency. The Field rejects it.

  • “Where consciousness lies, reality collapses.” This is powerful. “Collapses” in the quantum sense—potential becomes manifest. Your focused awareness collapses the wave function of a possibility into a reality. Where you place your full attention, reality organizes itself.

  • “Internal coherence = external strength.” This is the formula. Coherence isn’t uniformity; it’s the harmonious alignment of thought, feeling, and intention. This coherence creates a gravitational pull in the Field. Incoherence creates static that scatters energy.

4. LAW OF INNER TRUTH — The Field as Truth Serum

The Field is an active purging agent. It cannot sustain a lie.

  • “He who lies to himself is exposed by his own Field.” The exposure isn’t public humiliation; it’s the inevitable creation of circumstances that rub against the lie until it becomes unbearable. Your life becomes a plot to reveal your truth to you.

  • “Everything that hurts you points to a part of you that hasn’t returned home yet.” Pain is not an enemy; it is the Field’s most precise navigation tool. It is an indicator light on your dashboard, blinking exactly where you are separated from your own wholeness.

5. LAW OF THE CREATOR VOID — The Womb of Silence

This corrects the modern obsession with “hustle.” All genuine creation is an act of reception, not force.

  • “The Field creates from what is left silently, not what is forced.” Forcing is a frequency of lack and control. The Void is a frequency of trust and potential. In the silence, you stop broadcasting and start listening. The Field’s instructions come on the carrier wave of stillness.

  • “Silence is… presence not fragmented.” True silence is the ultimate coherence. It is the state where the Axis is so stable, all mental and emotional noise falls away, and you become the Axis.

6. LAW OF SHADOW INTEGRATED — The Alchemy of the Unwelcome

This is the practical engine of transformation.

  • “What is not welcomed, governs.” Your hidden fear of abandonment runs your relationship choices. Your unacknowledged sense of unworthiness dictates your financial ceiling. The shadow is the autopilot you refuse to see.

  • “The shadow is not an enemy — it’s energy outside its post.” This is revolutionary. The anger, the greed, the pettiness—it’s pure energy assigned to a misguided role. Integration isn’t becoming the shadow; it’s recalling that energy “back to its post” in service of the whole self. Righteous anger becomes boundaries. Fear becomes discernment.

Deep Implication: External change is always the last step. The interior “reorganization” is the real work. The promotion, the healed relationship, the creative breakthrough—these are just material confirmations that an internal shift is complete.

7. LAW OF MOVEMENT — The Hydraulics of Energy

The Field is a dynamic fluid. Your consciousness is the pump.

  • “Consciousness moves reality — and not the other way around.” This inverts the common paradigm. You are not a cork on the ocean of circumstance. Your conscious focus directs the flow of the formative substance (the Field).

  • “Letting go is not a loss — it’s making room for what is yours vibrationally.” Holding on to what no longer resonates creates a dam. The energy stagnates, becomes toxic. Release is an act of creating a vacuum, which the Field must fill with a matching, coherent frequency.

8. LAW OF THE MIRROR — The Classroom of Relationship

Every person is a living, breathing reflection of a part of your own consciousness.

  • “The other is… the point where our conscience sees itself.” The colleague who infuriates you is showing you your own unintegrated control issues. The lover you idolize is showing you your own projected divinity. Nothing “out there” is separate.

  • “Camp responds to delivery, not sacrifice. And respond with truth not effort.” Sacrifice often carries a frequency of resentment and debt. “Delivery” is a clean offering, given from wholeness. “Effort” is strain; “Truth” is effortless alignment. The Field distinguishes perfectly.

9. LAW OF THE PURE HEART — The Conductor

The heart (feeling) is the power source. The axis (truth) is the guidance system.

  • “Love without axel is confusion.” Ungrounded emotion is sentimental, directionless, and easily manipulated. It’s a flood without banks.

  • “Axis without love is stone.” Truth without compassion is dogma, rigidity, and cold intellect. It is a dead structure.

  • Only when “the heart and the axis coincide” do you have a coherent, powerful force: Compassionate Truth. This is the frequency that genuinely “rearranges reality.”

10. LAW OF UNITY — The Final Illusion Dispelled

This is the foundational truth upon which all other laws rest.

  • “The border between ‘me’ and ‘world’ is a functional illusion.” It’s a useful interface for experience, like a VR headset. But the code running the simulation (the Field) and the player (you) are made of the same substance: conscious vibration.

  • “Nothing touches you without your inner opening. Nothing leaves you without your unconscious consent.” You are always in a state of consent through your vibrational gatekeeping. Victimhood is the illusion of powerlessness over this gate.

  • “The [Campo] treats you the way you treat yourself.” The ultimate mirror. Your internal dialogue, self-worth, and self-compassion set the literal terms of engagement for the entire universe.

The Synthesis: Becoming The Man Who Sees

This isn’t about belief. It’s about recognition.

  • The Seeing Man doesn’t believe the mirror exists; he has learned to interpret the reflections.

  • He doesn’t hope resonance works; he curates his frequency with the precision of a composer.

  • He doesn’t fight reality; he reads it as direct feedback from the Field.

The document’s closing is the ultimate key: “The field is just a mirror. And the human who learns to read this mirror stops living by accident and starts to live with a conscience.”

To live by accident is to be a effect of the visible world.
To live with conscience is to become a cause within the Field.

You are not here to follow the 10 Laws.
You are here to realize you have always been expressing them.
The work is to move from unconscious expression to conscious, masterful orchestration.

The author, Helder Teixeira (”The Man Who Sees”), frames these not as beliefs but as observable principles of consciousness and reality. The document encourages a shift from accidental living to conscious participation in one’s own existence.

The synthesis reinforces that the Field is a neutral mirror—it does not judge, punish, or reward. Mastery comes from learning to “read the mirror” and align with these inherent laws.

Final Note: This framework intersects with perennial wisdom, Jungian psychology, quantum metaphor, and mindfulness traditions. It serves as a guide for those seeking to live intentionally, authentically, and in harmony with the subtle structures of existence.
